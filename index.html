Real Estate Sector Growth

The property sector of Melbourne has been on the rise for the past decade with a 26% increase in median property value since 2010. Some of the key forces pushing the prices can be a result of an increase in popultaion, change in loan interest rates or changes in building regulations for more high-rise buildings.






Metropolitan Melbourne

Depending on your budget range, the areas in the metropolitan region can vary in price. The price can be influenced by a number of factors such as proximity to recreational spaces,schools, heritage value and land size. The chart on the left shows the mean prices of each suburb in the metropolitan region between the years of 2016-2017. As shown, the value of the property is not as dependent on the proximity to the CBD as other possible factors. Typically the closer you are to the city the greater the value of the property.










Property Traits

One of the main traits that home buyers look at in a property are the number of bedrooms,bathrooms, car spaces and the landsize Each of these traits affect the price of the property as, it provides a greater potential of value in the present or future. The greater number of these traits result in a higher value for the property. A greater landsize allows for additional development such as subdividing the land, or building more townhouses to be rented out. Subdividing land is the result of dividing the inital land and selling that portion to the market.
